Privacy Policy
Pharmxtra-MTM App — Medication Therapy Management Platform

Effective Date: March 15, 2026 | Last Updated: March 15, 2026
Pharmxtra Pharmacy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Pharmxtra MTM mobile application ("App").

Service Area: This App is exclusively for registered patients and staff pharmacists of Pharmxtra Pharmacy, Lagos, Nigeria.
1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information
We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

Identity Information: Name, date of birth, gender, phone number, email address
Health Information: Medical conditions, allergies, current medications, prescription history
Clinical Data: Blood pressure readings, blood glucose levels, laboratory results (HbA1c, lipid panels, kidney/liver function tests)
Medication Adherence Data: Dose tracking, refill schedules, compliance records
Weight and BMI Data: Weight logs, height measurements
1.2 Automatically Collected Information
Device information (type, operating system)
App usage data and interaction logs
Error reports and performance data
1.3 AI-Processed Information
When you use our AI-powered features (Food Scanner, Health Insights), we may process:

Images of food items for nutritional analysis
Health data correlations for personalized insights
Drug interaction analysis based on your medication profile
Important: All AI-generated content is for informational purposes only and must be reviewed by a licensed pharmacist before any clinical decisions are made.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

Provide Medication Therapy Management (MTM) services
Track and improve medication adherence
Generate Comprehensive Medication Reviews (CMR)
Monitor health vitals and clinical outcomes
Identify potential drug interactions and safety concerns
Send medication reminders and refill alerts
Provide personalized health education content
Generate AI-powered health insights (with pharmacist review)
Communicate important health information
Improve our services and app functionality
3. Data Storage and Security
3.1 Local Storage
Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure browser storage mechanisms. This ensures you have access to your information even without internet connectivity.

3.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)
If you enable cloud sync features, your data may be stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase) with:

End-to-end encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
Encryption at rest (AES-256)
Regular security audits and monitoring
Access controls and authentication
3.3 Security Measures
Password-protected access for pharmacists
OTP verification for patient login
Session management and automatic logout
Regular backup reminders
4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do NOT sell your personal or health information. We may share your data only:

With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
With Healthcare Providers: Your assigned pharmacist and authorized staff
For AI Processing: With third-party AI services (OpenAI, Google Cloud Vision) for health insights — data is anonymized where possible
Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
5. Third-Party Services
Our App may use the following third-party services:

OpenAI GPT-4: For AI health insights and drug interaction analysis
Google Cloud Vision: For food/medicine image recognition
Supabase: For optional cloud storage and sync
Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

6. Your Rights
You have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of your personal data
Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
Export: Export your data in standard formats (JSON)
Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional features
Opt-Out: Disable AI features, cloud sync, or reminders
7. Data Retention
We retain your data:

Active Patients: For the duration of your enrollment in MTM services
Inactive Patients: Up to 7 years after last activity (for medical record compliance)
Deleted Accounts: 30 days grace period, then permanent deletion
8. Children's Privacy
This App is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
Updating the "Last Updated" date
Sending a notification through the App (for significant changes)
10. Nigeria Data Protection Compliance
This App complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. We ensure:

Lawful processing of personal data
Purpose limitation and data minimization
Accuracy and storage limitation
Integrity and confidentiality
Accountability and transparency
